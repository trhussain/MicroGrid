Power Sources: Don't button spam!

Main Grid (9V Battery)

The 9V battery represents the main utility grid in your microgrid model. It simulates standard grid power supplying your system under normal conditions. Toggle D19 to connect to the main grid, or simulate island mode by disconnecting it!

    Toggle D19

Generator (AA Battery Pack)

The AA battery pack models a backup generator in your microgrid. Generators provide power when solar or grid sources are unavailable. Use this to simulate backup power activation during outages or peak load periods. Toggle D21 to activate the generator.

  • Toggle D20

Solar Panel

The solar panel models a renewable energy source within your microgrid. It generates power during sunlight hours and reduces reliance on fossil fuels or the main grid. Toggle D21 to simulate solar generation and observe its effect on the system. Try shining a flashlight on this one when its on!

  • Toggle D21

Indicator LEDs!

An indicator LED will turn on when you toggle a line to tell you if its on or not!
